El IPN Sin Fronteras

This colloquium will offer a space for the exchange ideas adding to the discussion of the current approaches in the Internationalization of Higher Education and Research. Its objective is to harmonize the Instituto Politécnico Nacional with the global academic comunity through dialogue and the exchange of perspectives with experts to build a solid Internationalization process that allows the training of students, teachers nad researchers of global scale.
